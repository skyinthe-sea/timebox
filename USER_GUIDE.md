# Timebox Planner - User Guide

Timebox Planner helps you plan your day, stay focused, and track your productivity. Here's how to get started.

---

## Planner (Home)

**Brain Dump** - Quickly jot down everything you need to do. Type a task and tap submit. Past tasks appear as suggestions for faster entry.

**Top 3 Priorities** - Drag your most important tasks into the three priority slots (Gold, Silver, Bronze). These are highlighted on your calendar so you never lose sight of what matters most.

**Date Navigation** - Tap the arrows or the date to switch between days. A "Today" button appears when you're viewing a different date.

---

## Timeline (Calendar)

**Creating Time Blocks** - Long-press and drag on the timeline grid to select a time range. A popup lets you assign an existing task or create a new one for that slot.

**Moving & Resizing** - Drag a time block to move it. Drag the top or bottom edge to resize. Blocks snap to 30-minute intervals.

**Completing Tasks** - Swipe a block right to mark it complete. Swipe left to delete it (with confirmation). Tap a block for more status options.

**Focus Mode** - Tap the floating "Burning Time!" button to enter Focus Mode for the current active time block.

---

## Focus Mode

A full-screen countdown timer designed to keep you locked in.

- Shows the task name and remaining time with a circular progress ring.
- To prevent accidental exits, you must solve a quick math challenge to leave early.
- The session ends automatically when time is up.
- If no time block is active, you can start a quick session with preset durations (15/25/30/45/60 min).

---

## Statistics (Report)

View your productivity across **Daily**, **Weekly**, or **Monthly** periods:

- **Productivity Score** - An overall score (0-100) with trend tracking.
- **Completion Rings** - Task completion rate, time block completion rate, and time accuracy.
- **Plan vs Actual** - See how your estimated time compares to reality.
- **Focus Summary** - Total focus time, average session length, and longest streak.
- **Insights** - Smart recommendations based on your patterns (best focus times, streaks, etc.).

Pull down to refresh your data at any time.

---

## Settings

- **Theme** - Switch between System, Light, and Dark mode.
- **Language** - Choose from Korean, English, Japanese, Hindi, Chinese, French, or German.
- **Notifications** - Set start/end alarms for tasks and a daily planning reminder.
- **Time Preferences** - Configure your day's time range and default block duration.
- **Reset** - Restore all settings to defaults.

---

## Daily Workflow

1. **Dump** - Add all your tasks in the Brain Dump.
2. **Prioritize** - Pick your Top 3 for the day.
3. **Schedule** - Drag tasks onto the Timeline.
4. **Focus** - Use Focus Mode to work through each block.
5. **Review** - Check Statistics to see your progress and improve.

That's it. Plan it, block it, burn through it.
